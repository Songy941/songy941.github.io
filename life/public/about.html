If I Were You

If I Were You is a platform that let you experience life being other people in New York city. All stories were collected from people currently living in New York.



What's the purpose of this project?

The aim is for people to do and think from another person's perspective and build understanding and empathy towards the others.



Can I submit my experience?

Yes, you can submit through this link: https://www.surveymonkey.com/r/DPZFPVM
